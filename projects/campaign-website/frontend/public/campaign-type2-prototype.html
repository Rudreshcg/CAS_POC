The Procurement Challenges Apollo Solves

Six problems every Indian
chemical CPO recognises.

These are not hypothetical. They are the procurement challenges we hear consistently from chemical companies across Gujarat, Maharashtra, and Rajasthan — and the ones Apollo is specifically built to address.

📉
Commodity price movements that outpace your contracts
When soda ash, sulphur, or chlorine prices move, your fixed-price contracts don't. Competitors with indexed contracts capture the downside; you absorb it.
Apollo's Price Forecasting and Should-Cost agents give your team a live view of what commodities should cost before every contract renewal.
🇨🇳
Chinese import pressure your buyers can't quantify
Chinese producers have expanded capacity significantly across basic chemicals. Your domestic suppliers know this. Your buyers often don't have the data to use it as leverage.
Apollo's Procurement Prism maps global supply dynamics per material and arms your buyers with indexed benchmarks before they negotiate.
⚠️
Single-source dependencies that create production risk
Many specialty chemical inputs have limited qualified supplier pools. When a supplier has a quality event or logistics issue, your production line feels it within weeks.
Apollo's Alternate Suppliers and Resilience Sentinel agents continuously scan for qualified alternatives before you need them urgently.
🗂️
Material master data that no one fully trusts
Duplicate material codes, inconsistent descriptions, and fragmented vendor records mean your spend analysis is always slightly wrong — and your AI initiatives start on a broken foundation.
Apollo's Data De-duplication and Golden Records agents clean and consolidate your SAP or Oracle master data without a manual data migration project.
🤝
Negotiations where your buyers are outprepared
Your suppliers arrive with market data, cost justifications, and prepared positions. Your buyers arrive with last year's price and gut feel. The outcome of that meeting is predictable.
Apollo's Negotiation Assistant generates a complete intelligence brief — should-cost, market signals, supplier risk profile — automatically before every supplier meeting.
📊
Savings that get claimed but can't be verified
Procurement reports savings. Finance can't reconcile them to the P&L. Leadership loses confidence in the procurement function's numbers. This cycle repeats every quarter.
Apollo's Analytics agent tracks every saving to the purchase order level — verified, attributable, and reportable to the CFO in real time.
How Apollo Works

Not a replacement.
An intelligence layer.

Apollo sits alongside your existing ERP and procurement software. It does not replace SAP or Coupa. It reads your data, understands your context, and surfaces decisions your team hasn't had time to make.

01
Apollo connects to your ERP in 60–90 days
Our Forward Deployed Engineers configure Apollo to read from your SAP, Oracle, or Coupa environment via secure API. No data migration. No disruption to your team's existing workflows.
02
16 agents go to work on your spend
Each agent has a specific job — from cleaning your material master data to scanning global markets for better supplier alternatives. They run continuously, not just at contract renewal time.
03
Your team acts on intelligence, not instinct
Buyers see should-cost benchmarks before negotiations. CPOs see category strategies updated in real time. CFOs see savings tracked to the purchase order.
04
Your data never leaves your environment
Apollo runs inside your infrastructure — on-premise or private cloud. No spend data is shared externally, no models are trained on your information.
